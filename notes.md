## Biomes

No aquatic for now. Other kinds:

- Hot desert
- Dry shrubland
- Arctic desert
- Tundra
- Tropical dry forest
	- Deciduous to coniferous
- Tropical rainforest
- Temperate dry forest
	- Deciduous to coniferous
- Temperate rainforest
- Boreal forest
- Tropical grassland
- Temperate grassland
- Temperate Wetland
- Tropical Wetland
	- Mangroves
- Mountains

### Natural Features

An area might contain:

- Lakes
- Marshes
- Hills
	- Foothills

### Factors

Biomes are chosen based on:
- Latitude
- Neighbouring biomes
	- Precipitation
	- Type
	- Features
